Mission Statement

The basis of this project started with a problem the Museum of Ontario Archaeology Long house structure beging to slowly deteriorate. One of the oldest national momuments in Ontario. Longhouses structure are a major part of native Canadian history. In order to preserve it we decided to use artifical technology to rebuild some of the lost artifacts and stories let behind.

Meet the team a collection of talent from different corners of the earth. All gathered from one soul purpose! Passion.
