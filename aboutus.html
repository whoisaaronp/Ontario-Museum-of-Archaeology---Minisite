Mission Statement

Mission Statement

We started this project with the goal of offering visitors to the Lawson Site a unique and educational experience. We wanted to preserve the experience of viewing an authentic longhouse without depending on a physical structure.

We decided to use Augmented Reality technology to rebuild some of the lost artifacts and stories left behind.

Our Augmented Reality experience was built by a team of Fanshawe students in "Interactive Media Development - Data Visualization' along with their Professors. Meet the team a collection of talent from different corners of the earth. All gathered from one soul purpose! Passion.
