Learn about the Lawson sight AR experience

One of only a few village sites where earthworks are preserved The Lawson site is a 500-year-old village situated on a flat plateau overlooking Medway River and Snake Creek in northwest London. The Ontario Museum of Archaeology approached us at the Fanshawe College Data Visualization program with a problem they were having in terms of preserving their Longhouse sight. After some research on solving the problem, we decided to use artificial technology to create an experience where visitors can see a digital representation of the longhouse and other artifacts that is not accessible psychically to the public.
