Learn about the Lawson sight AR experience

One of only a few village sites where earthworks are preserved The Lawson site is a 500-year-old village situated on a flat plateau overlooking Medway River and Snake Creek in northwest London.

Combining history and technology creates a fresh, exciting, and enriched educational experience.

Augmented Reality offers a new layer of engagement and detail for our visitors. With the ability to interact with the artifacts in a virtual and accessible way, the visitor's experience is enriched. Kids love it, too!
